ABOUT ME

  My name is Rohan Pujari (RP). I am a professional photographer based in Pune, Maharashtra specializing in B.A.Photography. My photographs also got featured in a Kesari newspaper. I am currently pursuing my photography degree from TMV college, one of the best institutions for photography in India.

  I was pursuing a degree in BA after finishing my 12th grade but lost interest in it and chose to drop it after my first year of study. Eventually, I decided to pursue my passion for photography and got admitted to TMV college to earn a degree in photography.

  I first fell in love with photography in high school where I learned about the of photography and editing photographs. Although my camera has changed over the years it has always been my most prized possession. I love being able to capture memories and to capture the beauty of the world.

  As a photographer, I always try to create photographs that you will love and cherish for many years to come. I would be honored to capture part of your life. I hope, that you will enjoy with my photo portfolio and after that we can create something great together!
